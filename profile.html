I’m Robert Ciborowski, a university computer science student currently residing in Toronto. After becoming inspired by games for the PC and handheld consoles, I learned to code in my middle-school days. Throughout high school, I greatly advanced and refined my programming skills and picked up other skills, such as graphics design and music synthesis. During these times, I developed a hard-working attitude.

I started Neo-Zero near the end of high school and have developed it into its current state since then. Nearly all of the game’s mechanics have been programmed in and I plan on continuing my focus on level-design and story. This is my first large-scale game, and I have invested lots of my own time and money into it! I hope that Neo-Zero can bring a unique, touching experience to those that play it.
